# Electrician — Electrical Services

Similar to plumbing — skilled trade, strong unions, apprenticeship model. Compare directly to the plumber to see if different trades have different dynamics.

**Fundamental worker:** Electrician (journeyman / master)
**Customer cost proxy:** Average cost of an electrical service call, or average residential electrical work cost per job.

**Interesting angles:**
- Electrical work is more strictly licensed than most trades (for obvious safety reasons). Does the licensing create a moat that protects the ratio, or does it add overhead?
- Solar panel installation has created a new category of electrical work. New industry, new cost structure — is the ratio different for solar vs. traditional electrical?
- Compare to plumber: same general structure (union, apprenticeship, service calls). Same ratio trajectory?
