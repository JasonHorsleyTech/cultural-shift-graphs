# Roofer — Roofing

Physically demanding, dangerous work. Roofers have among the highest injury rates of any profession. A new roof costs $8,000-20,000. The roofing crew might spend 2-3 days on it.

**Fundamental worker:** Roofer
**Customer cost proxy:** Average cost of a roof replacement (HomeAdvisor, Angi, insurance claim data).

**Interesting angles:**
- Insurance-paid roofing (storm damage) vs. out-of-pocket: when insurance pays, prices inflate because the homeowner isn't price-sensitive. Does the roofer's share change when insurance is involved?
- Roofing is heavily immigrant labor. Has the labor cost stayed low while the total price has risen?
- Storm-chasing roofing companies that appear after hurricanes — pure overhead extraction? They hire subcontractors at low rates and charge insurance at high rates.
