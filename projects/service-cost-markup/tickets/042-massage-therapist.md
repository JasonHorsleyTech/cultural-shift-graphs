# Massage Therapist — Massage / Bodywork

Pure hands-on service. One therapist, one client, one hour. Very similar to therapy (ticket 010) in structure but with physical instead of mental health framing.

**Fundamental worker:** Licensed massage therapist (LMT)
**Customer cost proxy:** Average cost of a one-hour massage (spa, clinic, or independent).

**Interesting angles:**
- Massage Envy and similar franchise chains charge $70-80/session and pay the therapist $20-30. The franchise takes 60%+ of the service fee. The therapist is doing all the work.
- Independent massage therapists charging $80-120 keep most of it (minus room rental). The franchise model is the overhead.
- This is another good "control case" — minimal overhead, pure service. If the ratio is bad, it's because of the business model, not the industry.
