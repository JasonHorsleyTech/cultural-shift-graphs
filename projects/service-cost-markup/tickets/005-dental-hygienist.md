# Dental Hygienist — Dental Care

The person who actually cleans your teeth. You see the hygienist for 45 minutes and the dentist for 5. Shares a denominator with the dentist — interesting to compare who gets what share of the same visit cost.

**Fundamental worker:** Dental hygienist
**Customer cost proxy:** Cost of a routine dental cleaning/checkup visit (same denominator as dentist ticket).

**Interesting angles:**
- Hygienists do the bulk of preventive work. What fraction of a cleaning fee goes to them vs. the dentist who pops in for 5 minutes?
- Some states have expanded hygienist scope of practice — did that change the ratio?
