# Preparation ROI

For various levels of preparation (none, 30-day food supply, homestead, full prepper compound), how much does your survival probability change across apocalyptic scenarios?

## Why it's interesting

Answers the question "is prepping worth it or am I cosplaying?" Most prepper culture focuses on gear and supplies, but the actual ROI of preparation levels against different collapse scenarios has never been graphed. A 30-day food supply might buy you nothing in a nuclear winter but everything in a short grid-down event.

## Possible preparation tiers
- Level 0: Average American (3 days of food, city apartment)
- Level 1: Basic prep (30-day food/water, first aid, urban)
- Level 2: Moderate (rural property, garden, well water, 6-month supplies)
- Level 3: Serious (homestead, livestock, off-grid capable, community)
- Level 4: Full compound (self-sufficient, armed, multi-year supplies)
