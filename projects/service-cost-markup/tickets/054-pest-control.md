# Pest Control Technician — Extermination

The exterminator shows up with a sprayer and some chemicals. The service visit costs $100-300. The chemicals cost maybe $5-10 per application. The tech makes $15-20/hour and spends 30-60 minutes at your house. The math should be straightforward — but the franchise model might tell a different story.

**Fundamental worker:** Pest control technician / exterminator
**Customer cost proxy:** Average cost of a pest control visit (one-time or monthly service contract).

**Interesting angles:**
- Major franchise operations (Orkin, Terminix/Rentokil) vs. independent operators. Franchisees pay royalties, marketing fees, and buy chemicals from the franchisor at marked-up prices. The tech's share of what you pay shrinks at every layer.
- Termite treatment is the big-ticket item ($1,000-3,000+ for a house). The tech's time and chemicals are a small fraction. What's the rest? Liability insurance? Warranty reserves? Profit?
- Has the ratio changed with the consolidation of the industry? Terminix was bought by Rentokil (British company) for $2.7B in 2022.
