# Flight Attendant — Commercial Aviation

Same ticket price as the pilot ticket, different worker. The flight attendant is the service layer of flying — safety briefings, food, drinks, passenger management. They're paid far less than pilots for being on the same flight.

**Fundamental worker:** Flight attendant
**Customer cost proxy:** Same as pilot — average domestic airfare. The two lines against the same denominator will show how the airline allocates the fare dollar between its two primary customer-facing roles.

**Interesting angles:**
- Flight attendants are only paid from door-close to door-open. Boarding, deplaning, delays — unpaid. Their effective hourly rate is lower than their nominal rate.
- The glamour era (1960s-70s) vs. now — has the ratio changed with the shift from luxury to commodity?
- Union strength has varied wildly by airline and era. Correlation with ratio?
