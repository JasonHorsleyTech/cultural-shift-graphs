# Pharmacist — Pharmacy

The person who fills your prescription. Interesting because pharmacy is one of the most middleman-heavy industries: PBMs (pharmacy benefit managers) sit between the drug manufacturer, the pharmacy, the insurer, and the patient, extracting money at every step.

**Fundamental worker:** Pharmacist
**Customer cost proxy:** Average prescription cost (total — what insurance + patient pays combined). Or total prescription drug spending per capita.

**Interesting angles:**
- PBMs (Express Scripts, CVS Caremark, OptumRx) didn't exist before the 1980s. Did the pharmacist's ratio collapse after PBMs entered?
- Chain pharmacy (CVS, Walgreens) vs. independent — independent pharmacists probably had a better ratio historically
- Drug prices have exploded but pharmacist salaries have been relatively flat — the ratio is probably collapsing fast
