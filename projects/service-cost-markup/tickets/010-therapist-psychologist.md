# Therapist / Psychologist — Mental Health

Talk therapy is the purest form of "one skilled person gives you their expertise in a room." There is almost zero physical overhead. A therapist needs a chair, a box of tissues, and a quiet room. The ratio should theoretically be very high.

**Fundamental worker:** Licensed therapist (LCSW, LPC) and/or psychologist (PhD/PsyD)
**Customer cost proxy:** Average cost per therapy session (out-of-pocket + insurance payment).

**Interesting angles:**
- Therapists who take insurance earn dramatically less per session than those who don't. Two parallel ratio stories.
- The mental health demand explosion post-2020 — did that improve therapist leverage or just create burnout?
- Online therapy platforms (BetterHelp, Talkspace) — new middleman taking 30-40% of the session fee. Classic ratio compression.
